# AI & Automation Tools Stack

Tools I use in daily EA and operations work to reduce manual effort, increase accuracy, and give executives more time.

---

## 📬 Inbox & Email

| Tool | Use |
|---|---|
| **Fixer AI** | Automated email triage, follow-up tracking, priority flagging |
| **Gmail Filters** | Rule-based sorting into folders by sender, keyword, domain |
| **Boomerang / Streak** | Scheduled sends, follow-up reminders, email tracking |

---

## 📅 Calendar & Scheduling

| Tool | Use |
|---|---|
| **Google Calendar** | Primary calendar — color-coded by type (meetings, deep work, personal) |
| **Calendly** | External scheduling without back-and-forth |
| **Notion / Asana** | Two-week planning board, travel itineraries, project timelines |

---

## 📋 CRM & Lead Management

| Tool | Use |

## 📁 Project & Task Management

---

## Philosophy

I use tools to eliminate repetitive decisions, not to replace judgment. Every automation I set up has a clear owner, a clear trigger, and a human review point for anything that matters. The executive sees the output, not the machinery.
