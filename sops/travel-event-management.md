# SOP: Travel & Event Management

**Owner:** EA / Operations Lead  
**Applies to:** Business travel, client events, conferences, and off-site meetings

---

## Overview

Every trip and event is managed end-to-end by the EA. The executive receives a single briefing document before departure and has a live shared doc for real-time updates. Zero logistics questions during travel.

---

## Pre-Trip Timeline

| Timeframe | Action |
|---|---|
| 3+ weeks out | Confirm dates, purpose, attendees. Book flights and accommodation. |
| 2 weeks out | Book ground transport, confirm hotel details, research local venues. |
| 1 week out | Build full Trip Briefing Sheet (see template below). Share via Notion/Asana. |
| 48 hours out | Confirm all bookings. Send reminder to attendees. Print or download offline copies. |
| Day before | Final check: weather, local news, any changes. Update exec via WhatsApp. |

---

## Trip Briefing Sheet — Standard Format

Each trip gets a single-page document with the following structure:

```
TRIP: [Destination] | [Purpose]
DATES: [Departure] → [Return]
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FLIGHTS
  Outbound: [Airline] [Flight#] | Departs [Time] | Arrives [Time]
  Return:   [Airline] [Flight#] | Departs [Time] | Arrives [Time]
  Confirmation: [#]

ACCOMMODATION
  [Hotel Name] | [Address]
  Check-in: [Date/Time] | Check-out: [Date/Time]
  Confirmation: [#] | Phone: [#]

GROUND TRANSPORT
  [Service] | Pickup: [Time/Location] | Driver: [Name] | Phone: [#]

MEETINGS & EVENTS
  [Day, Date]
  09:00 — [Meeting/Event] | [Address] | Contact: [Name, Phone]
  14:00 — [Meeting/Event] | [Address] | Contact: [Name, Phone]

KEY CONTACTS
  [Name] | [Role] | [Phone] | [Email]

EMERGENCY CONTACTS
  Hotel front desk: [#]
  Airline: [#]
  EA (me): [#]

NOTES
  [Dress code, dietary restrictions at venue, local customs, etc.]
```

---

## Live Shared Document

For multi-day trips or events with multiple stakeholders, a shared Notion or Asana page is created with:

- Real-time itinerary (editable)
- Vendor contact list
- Document links (confirmations, tickets)
- Task list for pre/during/post trip
- Comment thread for updates

All relevant parties have view or edit access depending on their role.

---

## Post-Trip

Within 24 hours of return:

- [ ] File all expense receipts and submit expense report
- [ ] Update CRM with any contacts made during the trip
- [ ] Send thank-you emails to hosts/clients if applicable
- [ ] Debrief with executive: what worked, what to improve next time
- [ ] Archive trip folder with all documents

---

## Event Management (Conferences / Client Events)

Same structure as travel, plus:

- Attendee list with contact info
- Registration confirmations
- AV/tech requirements confirmed with venue
- Speaker or presenter briefing (if applicable)
- Day-of run sheet with minute-by-minute timing
- Backup plan for key logistics (transport, AV, catering)
