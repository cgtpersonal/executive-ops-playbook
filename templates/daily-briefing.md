→ [One sentence: the single most important thing today]

TODAY'S CALENDAR
  [Time] — [Meeting/Event] | [Location/Link] | [Who]
  [Time] — [Meeting/Event] | [Location/Link] | [Who]
  [Time] — Deep work block (protected)

  • [Subject] — [1-line summary + what decision is needed]
  • [Subject] — [1-line summary + what decision is needed]

LEADS TO WATCH
  • [Lead Name / Company] — [Status / next action today]
